The Lion king

Lion is a feline of the genus Panthera and the world's second largest cat.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 14 years (Adult)
  • Average Length: 1.4 - 2 meters
  • Speed: 80 km/h

Lions are large felines that are traditionally depicted as the "king of the jungle". These big cats once roamed Africa, Asia and Europe. However, now they are found in only two areas of the world. The lion typically inhabits grasslands and savannahs.
